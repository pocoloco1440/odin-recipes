Description

Fried plantains are a quick and delicious snack made from ripe plantains sliced and cooked until golden brown. They're naturally sweet, soft on the inside, and slightly crispy on the edges, making them a perfect side dish for many West African meals.

This simple recipe uses only a few ingredients and takes just a few minutes to prepare. Whether you serve them with rice, stews, or enjoy them on their own, fried plantains add a warm, caramelized flavor that's hard to resist.
