Egg Sandwich

Home

Description

Egg sandwiches are one of the simplest comfort foods you can make, but they still deliver big flavor with almost no effort. Whether you're rushing out the door or just want something warm and satisfying, this recipe gives you a quick, reliable option that always hits the spot. The combination of soft eggs, toasted bread, and a little seasoning creates a balanced bite that feels both familiar and comforting.

What makes an egg sandwich great is how customizable it is. You can keep it classic with just eggs and bread, or add cheese, butter, veggies, or sauces to match your mood. It's a flexible recipe that works for breakfast, lunch, or a late-night snack, and it comes together in just a few minutes. Simple ingredients, fast prep, and a delicious result, that's the whole charm.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 egg
  • A pinch of salt
  • pinch of pepper
  • A little butter or oil

Steps

  1. Heat a pan and fry the egg with salt and pepper.
  2. Toast or warm the bread lightly.
  3. Place the egg between the slices of bread.
  4. Press gently, slice if you want, and serve.
